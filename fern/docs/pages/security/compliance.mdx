## Medical Disclaimer

The SafeRx Drug Safety API provides drug safety information compiled from published pharmaceutical sources. It is intended as a clinical decision support tool.

**The API does not constitute medical advice.** Safety data should be reviewed by a licensed pharmacist or physician before clinical decisions are made. SafeRx is not a substitute for professional clinical judgment.

## Egyptian Regulatory Context

SafeRx drug data covers products registered with the Egyptian Drug Authority (EDA). Product information including registered trade names, active ingredients, manufacturers, and pricing reflects the Egyptian pharmaceutical market.

## Data Accuracy

Drug safety data is compiled from:
- Regulatory drug labels and safety warnings
- Published drug interaction databases
- International dose reference standards
- Clinical literature and validation studies
