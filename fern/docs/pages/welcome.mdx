The SafeRx Drug Safety API provides real-time drug safety screening for Egyptian pharmaceuticals. Submit drug names, get back comprehensive safety data across six clinical domains in a single response.

## What You Can Do

<Cards>
  <Card title="Adverse Effects" icon="fa-solid fa-triangle-exclamation">
    Black Box Warnings, severe and common side effects, monitoring parameters, and dose-response classification for 28,557 products with 98.2% coverage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Drug Interactions" icon="fa-solid fa-arrows-split-up-and-left">
    337,000+ interaction pairs with severity scoring (Minor → Contraindicated), clinical descriptions, and monitoring recommendations in English and Arabic.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pregnancy & Lactation" icon="fa-solid fa-baby">
    0–7 risk scale for both pregnancy and lactation across 24,512 mapped products, with clinical rationale and Black Box Warning data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Food Interactions" icon="fa-solid fa-utensils">
    38,000+ food-drug interactions including meal timing recommendations, food conflict severity, and Egyptian-specific food considerations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clinical Considerations" icon="fa-solid fa-stethoscope">
    Population-specific safety data (pediatric, geriatric, cardiac, hepatic, renal) and condition-drug interaction alerts for 14 comorbidities.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dosing" icon="fa-solid fa-pills">
    Dual-source dosing safety with international reference standards and regulatory label data. 19,000+ products with max daily dose calculations and combo drug handling.
  </Card>
</Cards>

## How It Works

```
POST /api/drug_safety/check
    │
    ├── Drug Resolution (fuzzy matching)
    │
    ├── Parallel Safety Screening
    │   ├── Adverse Effects
    │   ├── Drug Interactions
    │   ├── Pregnancy & Lactation
    │   ├── Food Interactions
    │   ├── Clinical Considerations
    │   └── Dosing
    │
    ├── Alert Generation (critical items bubbled up)
    │
    └── Response (~40ms average)
```
